Sydney Mclaughin

Sydney McLaughlin is an American hurdler and sprinter who competed for the University of Kentucky before turning professional. McLaughlin holds a number of age group world bests and won the Gatorade National Girls Athlete of the Year trophy for both 2015 through 2016 and 2016 through 2017.
